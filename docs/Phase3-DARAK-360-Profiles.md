# Phase 3 — DARAK 360 Profiles

DARAK 360 Profiles convert scattered operational records into clear management views.

## Resident 360

Resident 360 answers: what is the complete story of this resident?

It combines occupancy, current unit, outstanding financial exposure, maintenance/support activity, conversations, notifications, legal pressure, risk flags, and recommended next actions.

## Unit 360

Unit 360 answers: what is the complete story of this unit?

It combines occupancy history, current resident, financial exposure, meters, maintenance, work orders, visitor load, readiness records, damage liabilities, turnover blockers, and recommended next actions.

## Compound 360

Compound 360 answers: what is the operational state of this compound?

It combines inventory, residents, active occupancy, finance, operations, legal/risk, communications, and high-level management signals.

## Commercial value

This feature makes DARAK easier to sell and operate because management can move from raw modules to clear profile-level decision views.
