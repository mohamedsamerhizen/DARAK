# Phase 5 — SaaS + Prioritization Brain

Phase 5 adds a commercial intelligence layer that helps an operator or buyer see DARAK as a managed SaaS product rather than only a feature-rich backend.

## Capabilities

1. **SaaS portfolio overview**
   - Accessible compounds only.
   - License status and capacity usage.
   - Tenant priority bands.

2. **Tenant readiness**
   - Finance exposure.
   - Legal/collection blockers.
   - Operations and support workload.
   - Notification and integration reliability.

3. **DARAK prioritization brain**
   - Produces ranked actions by area.
   - Assigns recommended owners.
   - Suggests next steps and due dates.

## Commercial value

This turns DARAK from a transaction system into an executive operating layer suitable for sales demos, tenant onboarding, and multi-compound portfolio governance.
